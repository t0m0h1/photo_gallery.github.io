A bit about myself:

A warm welcome to my page!

I am a drone photogrpher and computer science student from Boston Spa, West Yorkshire.

The thing that fascinates me most about drones is their versatility; they can remotely capture stunning quality videos while using their sensors to avoid obstacles, use GPS to navigate themselves, and hold still in winds upwards of 40mph!

That's just the beginning, you can learn more with my article on drones here.

My intention is to provide you with a fresh perspective on the world. I strive to capture those awe-inspiring shots that leave you speechless (although sometimes that can be quite difficult with the scenery near me!), and anything that effectively tells a visual story of my subject.

Things I love to capture:

  • ground textures
  • natural landscapes
  • old and grand buildings
  • beautiful UK coastlines
  • long exposure photogrphy
  • short videos / reels
